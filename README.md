## Project Overview
-Smart Queue & Crowd Management System
A web-based queue management system designed to reduce crowd congestion and save students’ time in places like payment counters and inquiry desks. Students can scan a QR code to join a virtual queue, enter their details and purpose, and receive a digital token instantly.
The admin dashboard allows staff to monitor all queue requests in real time, call students one by one, track ongoing processes, and send notifications such as “Your Turn” or “Almost Your Turn.” This creates a smooth, organized, and contactless queue experience without long physical waiting lines.
